Bill columns
title: string
date: timestamp
status: 1-7:
    1 first reading (stage 1)
    2 second reading (stage 1)
    3 third reading (stage 1)
    4 first reading (stage 2)
    5 second reading (stage 2)
    6 third reading (stage 2)
    7 Passed
    Note that stage 1 is senate if this is a S-bill, and house of commons if it's a C-bill
bill_type: 'S' | 'C' (e.g. S)
bill_id: string (e.g. S-6)
bill_num: int (e.g. 6)
